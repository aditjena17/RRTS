About Us

Welcome to the Road Repair and Tracking System (RRTS), an advanced software solution designed to modernize and automate road repair management for the city's Public Works Department.

Our Vision

To create a smart city where road repairs are handled efficiently through a seamless, data-driven digital platform.

What We Do

  • Repair Request Management: Residents report issues; clerks log them into the system.
  • Inspection & Prioritization: Supervisors assess road conditions and set repair priorities.
  • Resource Allocation: The system estimates materials, workforce, and machine requirements.
  • Automated Scheduling: Repair tasks are scheduled based on priority and available resources.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Officials access reports on repairs, workforce, and resources.

Why Choose RRTS?

  • Efficiency: Reduces manual work and optimizes processes.
  • Transparency: Provides real-time repair status updates.
  • Accountability: Ensures repairs are prioritized effectively.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Enables authorities to track progress and allocate resources wisely.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to transforming urban infrastructure maintenance, ensuring a smarter, safer, and more efficient road repair system.
